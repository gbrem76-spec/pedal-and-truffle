The Experience

Adventure Made Effortless

Most people will never ride the Attack of the Buns route. We've changed that.

The Attack of the Buns is a 320km bikepacking legend — celebrated for its breathtaking Southern Tablelands scenery, remote wilderness, and unforgettable gravel roads. Designed for serious bikepackers, it demands days of hard effort and expert navigation.

Pedal & Truffle takes the magic of this iconic route and opens it to everyone. With premium electric bikes, an expert local guide, and a curated overnight stay at the extraordinary Charley's Forest Truffles, this is adventure travel at its finest — without the suffering.

Your journey begins at Bungendore train station — accessible from Sydney or Canberra — and finishes at the South Coast in Nowra, with every detail handled for you.

Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Do I need cycling experience? +
No experience is needed. Our premium electric bikes do the hard work on hills and headwinds. If you can ride a bicycle, you can do this tour. Our guide sets a comfortable pace for the group and the route is graded to suit all fitness levels.
How do I get to Bungendore? +
Bungendore is easily accessible by train from Sydney (via Canberra) and Canberra. This is one of the best features of the tour — no car required. Check NSW TrainLink for schedules. We meet you at the station platform.
How do I get home from Nowra? +
Nowra has good transport links south to the coast and north back to Sydney. Coaches run regularly and we can advise on the best connection for your plans. We drop you at the Nowra transport hub at the end of Day 2.
What should I bring? +
We provide everything bike-related. You'll need comfortable riding clothes, a change of clothes for the evening at Charley's Forest Truffles, sunscreen, and a light daypack. A full packing list is sent with your booking confirmation.
When do tours run? +
We run tours from March through November — the ideal riding season in the Southern Tablelands. Seasonal departures are listed on our booking page. Private tours can be scheduled outside these dates on request. Contact us for group availability.
Is the route related to Attack of the Buns? +
Yes — our tour is inspired by the celebrated Attack of the Buns bikepacking route, which runs from Bungendore to Bundanoon (via Nowra). We follow key sections of this award-winning trail, passing through the same spectacular scenery that made the original route famous, but with the support and comfort that makes it accessible to all.
